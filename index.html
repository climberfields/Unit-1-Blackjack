Welcome fans of all ages!

Unless you're my brother, this isn't your Dad's BlackJack. This is BlackJack for Marvel and DC fans. A clash of the titans! Your objective is to win by either getting 21 or as close to it, while beating the dealer. You will be dealt two cards initially then you will have the option of either hitting (get another card) or stay (pass the move to the dealer). If the dealer gets 21, they win. If you both tie, then it's a draw. If the dealer has less points than you or goes over 21, you win.
